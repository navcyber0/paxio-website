Paxio Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Paxio ("we," "us," "the app") is a parental control app that helps a parent manage a child's Android device — screen time, app blocking, and content filtering. This policy explains what data Paxio collects, why, and where it goes. Paxio is installed and configured by a parent or guardian; it does not create a separate account for the child.

Data we collect

DataPurposeWhere it's stored
Parent email (Google or email sign-in)Identify the parent account; sign in on a new deviceFirebase Authentication (Google)
Child profile (name, age, avatar, daily limit)Personalize the dashboard; survive a reinstallFirebase Firestore (Google), tied to the parent account
Parent PINProtect settings from being changed by the childSalted PBKDF2 hash only — never plaintext
App usage duration, per appShow progress; enforce daily limitsLocal device database only — never uploaded
Blocked/allowed app listEnforce app blockingLocal device database only — never uploaded
DNS requests, while filtering is onBlock sites matching selected categoriesProcessed on-device via local VPN; never sent anywhere
Foreground app package nameDetect when a blocked app opensRead in memory on-device only
Crash & diagnostic reportsFind and fix bugsFirebase Crashlytics (Google)

What we don't collect

Paxio does not collect location data, does not read screen content, keystrokes, messages, photos, or browsing history, and does not use advertising or third-party analytics SDKs.

Data sharing

We don't sell data or share it with advertisers. Data above is processed by Google/Firebase solely to provide sign-in, profile sync, and crash reporting, under Google's own privacy terms.

Data retention & deletion

Child profile and PIN data are kept until the parent deletes the child profile or account. Local, on-device data is removed when Paxio is uninstalled. To request deletion, contact us below.

Children's privacy

Paxio is installed and configured by a parent or guardian. The child does not create an account, sign in, or provide information directly to Paxio.

Permissions Paxio requests

Accessibility Service, Usage Access, Device Admin, and VPN are requested with an in-app explanation before use, for app blocking, screen-time tracking, uninstall protection, and content filtering respectively.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: support@paxio.in.
