Delete Your Paxio Account

Last updated: July 2026

This page explains how to delete your Paxio parent account and the data associated with it. Account deletion is self-service, directly in the app — there is no support-ticket or email-request process for this.

Delete your account in the app

  1. Open Paxio and enter your parent PIN.
  2. Go to the Settings tab.
  3. Scroll to the Danger Zone section and tap Delete Account.
  4. Review the warning and tap Delete to confirm.

Deletion happens immediately — your account, child profiles, and cloud data are removed right away, no waiting period. If Paxio asks you to sign in again first, this is a Google security requirement for account deletion (it only appears if you haven't signed in recently); sign back in and repeat the steps above.

What gets deleted

DataOutcome
Parent account (email, authentication record)Permanently deleted from Firebase Authentication
Child profile(s) (name, age, avatar, daily limit, bedtime settings)Permanently deleted from Firestore
Parent PIN (stored only as a salted hash)Permanently deleted from Firestore
Screen-time usage history, app-blocking lists, content-filter rules (stored locally on the device)Permanently cleared from the device when you delete in-app; also removed if you uninstall the app

What is not affected

Anonymized crash/diagnostic reports already sent to Firebase Crashlytics prior to your deletion request may be retained by Google under their own standard retention period, as they are not linked back to your account identity.
