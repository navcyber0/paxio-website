Delete Your Paxio Account

Last updated: July 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your Paxio parent account and the data associated with it.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to support@paxio.in from the email address associated with your Paxio account.
  2. Use the subject line "Delete my Paxio account".
  3. Include the email address you sign in with, so we can locate your account.
Email support@paxio.in

We process deletion requests within 7 days and will confirm by email once complete.

What gets deleted

DataOutcome
Parent account (email, authentication record) Permanently deleted from Firebase Authentication
Child profile(s) (name, age, avatar, daily limit, bedtime settings) Permanently deleted from Firestore
Parent PIN (stored only as a salted hash) Permanently deleted from Firestore

What is not affected

Screen-time usage history and app-blocking lists are stored only locally on the device itself, never uploaded to our servers — deleting your account does not touch this data. Uninstalling the app from the device removes it. Anonymized crash/diagnostic reports already sent to Firebase Crashlytics prior to your deletion request may be retained by Google under their own standard retention period, as they are not linked back to your account identity.

Contact

Questions about account deletion can be sent to support@paxio.in.
